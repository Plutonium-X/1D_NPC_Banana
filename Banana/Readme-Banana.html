1.       WELCOME

Banana! is an original NPC Mod who will join your party and probably scare the crap out of you.

2.       Installation

To install,  simply run the installer (Setup-Banana.exe) and follow the instructions. To uninstall, run the installer again and choose the “Uninstall” option.

3.       FAQs

Q: Where can I find Banana! ?
A: Like many token NPCs, you can find Banana! at the Copper Coronet. You can't miss her; she's bright yellow.

Q: What the hell....what is this?! What kind of sick people are you?
A: Banana! is WeiDU and is designed solely for the purpose of getting a smile on your face.

Q: Will Banana! join my party?
A: If you allow it, yes. But be careful: fruit is unpredictable.

Q: Romance?
A: Banana! has had a pretty rough life, so probably not. For more info, check out her biography!

Q: Banana! is broken.
A: Email one of the authors above and we'll help you out.
